About me

I received the M.ED degree in language and literacy from Harvard University. I am interested in how home and school practices shape children's early language and literacy development. My current research explores 1) how teachers’ shared-book reading strategies influence children's vocabulary development in rural China preschools; 2) the development of research-based tools to evaluate classroom interaction in underserved areas. I am also co-founder of Button Baby, an educational startup that aims at bringing simple and effective interactions to families.

Research interest: early language and literacy, bilingual education, parenting, educational media
